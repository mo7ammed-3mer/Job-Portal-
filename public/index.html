Just Search Just Find with Job Portal App

The purpose of this App is to help employers and job seekers find each other in a simpler and new age way. It is an online Job Portal plan. To encourage job seekers to apply for jobs offered and find suitable jobs while companies post job vacancies and find good candidates.

How App Works

Knowing How the Application Works

The Job Portal App works using the searching algorithm. It uses a database system that stores the information of employers (companies), and job seekers, as the information required for each party. The employer offers jobs, then thejob seeker applies for jobs.
